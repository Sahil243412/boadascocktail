About Boada’s Cocktails

Boada’s Cocktails combines creative mixology with an energetic nightlife experience. Located in Carrer dels Tallers, 1, Barcelona, we use premium spirits, fresh ingredients, and bold flavor combinations to create unique cocktails. Our atmosphere blends sleek design, upbeat music, and a welcoming vibe for every guest.
